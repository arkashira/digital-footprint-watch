## Customer Segments
- **Tech‑savvy professionals & freelancers** (ages 25‑45) who maintain public profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, personal blogs, and social media – estimated 12 M potential users in the US & SE‑Asia.  
- **Small‑to‑medium SaaS companies** (10‑200 employees) that need to monitor employee‑exposed data for brand risk – ~8 k firms in the US, ~15 k in Thailand.  
- **Privacy‑conscious consumers** in emerging markets (Indonesia, Vietnam, Thailand) seeking a simple “one‑click” footprint audit – ~30 M internet users with high mobile adoption.

## Value Propositions
- **Automated, cross‑platform footprint scanning** (GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter, personal domains) with daily alerts – reduces manual search time by ≈ 90 %.  
- **Risk scoring & remediation suggestions** (e.g., exposed API keys, outdated contact info) powered by our in‑house LLM fine‑tuned on 21 M code‑and‑message pairs.  
- **Privacy‑first architecture** – all scans run locally or in a secure EU/TH data center; no raw data is stored, complying with GDPR & PDPA.  
- **Developer‑friendly API** for integration into CI/CD pipelines, enabling “shift‑left” security checks.  
- **Free community tier** with 5‑site monitoring; premium tier adds unlimited sites, historical trend analytics, and team dashboards.

## Channels
- **Self‑serve SaaS portal** (web & mobile) – primary acquisition via SEO, content marketing, and developer forums (Stack Overflow, Reddit r/privacy).  
- **Marketplace listings** – GitHub Marketplace, AWS Marketplace, and Thai cloud provider (TrueCloud) storefronts.  
- **Partner integrations** – bundled with existing Axentx products (e.g., AI‑red‑team service) and third‑party password managers (1Password, LastPass).  
- **Webinars & workshops** targeting corporate HR & security teams in Bangkok, Singapore, and San Francisco.  

## Customer Relationships
- **Freemium → Premium conversion funnel** with in‑app usage nudges and automated ROI calculators.  
- **Dedicated success managers** for enterprise accounts (≥ $2 k/mo) – quarterly health reviews and custom alert rule workshops.  
- **Community support** via Discord & GitHub Discussions; open‑source SDKs encourage peer‑to‑peer troubleshooting.  
- **Feedback loop** integrated into the product (monthly NPS survey) feeding directly into the BRAIN for continuous improvement.
