### Rationale for Selection

1. **Clearbit**: Provides valuable business data that can enhance user profiles, making it easier for users to understand their digital footprint in a business context. The affiliate program allows for potential revenue sharing.

2. **FullContact**: Offers extensive social profile aggregation, which is crucial for users wanting to manage their online presence effectively. The affiliate program provides additional monetization opportunities.

3. **Hunter.io**: Helps users find and verify emails, which can be essential for personal branding and outreach. The free-tier allows for initial testing without costs.

4. **Zapier**: While it doesn't offer a revenue-share model, its ability to automate workflows across various platforms adds significant value to users looking to streamline their digital footprint management.

5. **Pipl**: This API allows users to search for personal information, which is critical for understanding and managing one's digital presence. The affiliate program enhances the potential for revenue.

6. **Social Search**: Although it lacks a revenue-share model, it provides essential monitoring capabilities for social media, which is a key aspect of managing a digital footprint.

7. **BrandYourself**: This service focuses on online reputation management, which aligns perfectly with the product's goal of helping users manage their digital footprint. The affiliate program offers additional revenue opportunities.

8. **Google Alerts**: While not a traditional API, integrating Google Alerts can help users stay informed about their digital presence. It adds value without any associated costs.

### Conclusion

The integration of these partners will enhance the functionality of Digital Footprint Watch, providing users with comprehensive tools to manage their online presence effectively. Prioritizing partners with affiliate programs will also create additional revenue streams for the product.
